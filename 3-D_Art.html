3-D ART

Recently I started learning 3D art not for any particular reason other than I thought It would be cool. I started with YouTube tutorials, the first thing I built was a doughnut then after that I made a gem with a structure to hold it. I should mention that the structure that the blue gem is hovering over was from a free package I got online. Unlike other art forms 3D arts can download components of a scene like a chair or a table rather than making everything by hand. I have worked in these 3D software environments

  • Blender
  • Maya
  • Cinema 4D
